Our Story

The Intelligence Layer Healthcare Has Been Missing

Healthcare organizations collect enormous amounts of data, but most of it is fragmented across dozens of systems and rarely translates into action at the point of care.

Persivia was built to change that.

We created CareSpace, a digital health operating platform that unifies data from EHRs, claims, labs, SDOH sources, remote monitoring, and more into a continuously updated longitudinal patient record. On top of that foundation sits Persivia’s AI engine, care management workflows, and quality reporting tools, giving every team, from the C-suite to the care manager, the intelligence they need to act.

Today health systems, ACOs, payers, and risk-bearing provider groups rely on Persivia to close care gaps at scale, succeed in value-based contracts, and improve cost and quality across the full continuum of care, often replacing five or six disconnected point solutions with one unified platform.
